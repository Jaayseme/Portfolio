About Me

Hey there...My Name is David John Audu. I am from Borno State. I am a graduate of Civil Engineering from the Prestigious University of Maiduguri. I am a Writer and Author of some books which has been a blessing to people. Basically, I teach about Jesus through the help of the Holy Spirit and I trust God that you encounter Jesus as you read my books. Once again, Welcome to my page. God bless you!
